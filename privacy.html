Privacy Policy

LabelForge, a Multiclass Studio product · Effective July 2, 2026

The app

The LabelForge app collects nothing. It runs entirely on your Mac, sandboxed, with no network entitlement, meaning macOS itself prevents it from making network connections. Your labels, addresses, and tracking numbers are processed locally and never leave your machine. The app has no accounts, no analytics, no telemetry, and no crash reporting.

This website

  • No analytics, no tracking cookies, no ad tech.
  • The site is hosted on Vercel, which keeps standard server access logs (IP address, user agent, pages requested) for operations. See Vercel's privacy policy.
  • Downloads are served by GitHub Releases. See GitHub's privacy statement.
  • Fonts load from Google Fonts, which logs font requests. See Google Fonts privacy FAQ.
